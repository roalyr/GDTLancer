# TRUTH LORE CONSTRAINTS

**Version:** 2.0  
**Date:** 2026-07-24  
**Status:** Approved Lore Rules (Digital Board Game)

---

## Pillar 1: The Human Nucleus & No Institutions

The setting represents a raw, isolated frontier. There are no sprawling galactic corporations or sweeping empires. Society is localized to individual infrastructure nodes and tight-knit communities. Space is a hostile backdrop framing fragile human collectives.

#### What This Prohibits
- ❌ UI/Tags using terms like "corporation", "empire", or "military fleet".
- ❌ Treating stations as generic hubs. Each is a specific community board.

#### What This Requires
- ✅ Factions are small clans, families, or local alliances.
- ✅ Board interactions focus on personal grudges, favors, and survival.

---

## Pillar 2: The Weight of Travel & Vessel Demystification

Spaceflight is a non-trivial, perilous expedition demanding logistical sacrifice. Ships are not everyday cars.
- **Ownership:** Vessels are primarily community-owned (the default), though vessel-colonies and privately-owned ships exist as rare exceptions.
- **Scarce Command:** Pilots and captains are rare and highly consequential roles.

#### What This Prohibits
- ❌ Mechanics allowing trivial, consequence-free fast travel.
- ❌ Board states where every NPC is a pilot.

#### What This Requires
- ✅ Travel actions heavily tax resources and time (World Clock ticks).
- ✅ Vessel destruction or damage permanently mutates the board state and character availability.

---

## Pillar 3: Community-Centric (and Loner) Progression

Progression strips away linear gear-score grinding, focusing instead on community health, interpersonal dependencies, and resource cache management. 
- **Viability:** While community is integral, **loner roleplay must be viable** for players who choose isolation.

#### What This Prohibits
- ❌ Loot-drop gear scaling (e.g., "Level 5 Laser").
- ❌ Victory states tied solely to personal wealth accumulation.

#### What This Requires
- ✅ Board states visually reflect the health and stability of the sector.
- ✅ Progression is tied to manipulating board tracks, acquiring impactful tags, and surviving the World Clock.

## CURRENT GOAL: Remote Contract Board And Shared Contract Accounting
- TARGET_SCOPE: Replace the station-owned player contract interface with a global Contract Board reachable from DebugWindow, then harden the live runtime delivery loop so contract cargo pickup and reward/specie payout are backed by bounded qualitative source/payment accounting for both player and NPC agents. Conservation is preserved by treating `Accept` as reservation only, `Pick Up` as consumption of one reserved source-side cargo unit into carried contract cargo, and `Complete` as consumption of one reserved target-side payment/specie bundle before reward payout through the existing HUD-facing credit path. Player contract actions become explicit `Accept`, `Pick Up`, and `Complete` board interactions gated by sector presence rather than docking, while the existing qualitative demand-tag -> runtime-occurrence -> relief-impact simulation pipeline remains intact.

## CURRENT GOAL: Station Market Periodic Restocking

- TARGET_SCOPE: Station market inventories (both authored `.tres` locations and procedurally generated plain-dict discovered locations) currently deplete permanently through NPC and player buy/sell trades with no recovery path. Over an extended session, all commodity quantities will hit zero and dock-trade will silently no-op for all agents. This milestone adds a bounded, qualitative tick-driven restocking pass that incrementally restores each commodity quantity in every station's `market_inventory` toward its seeded baseline level. Restocking must be deterministic from each station's seeded baseline (already stored in `GameState.locations`), must respect the same `{commodity_id: {buy_price: int, sell_price: int, quantity: int}}` shape already in use, and must fire at a per-tick cadence driven by an integer constant so that the rate is tunable without architectural change. This milestone does NOT introduce per-sector price curves, dynamic pricing, supply/demand imbalances, faction-owned restock rates, or any change to the serialization or market shape already established.
- TARGET_FILES:
  - src/core/simulation/agent_layer.gd — owns the `_tick_market_restock(...)` helper that runs once per simulation tick against `GameState.locations`, incrementing each commodity quantity toward its baseline cap by a fixed step, for both authored and discovered station entries.
  - src/autoload/Constants.gd — add `MARKET_RESTOCK_RATE_PER_TICK: int` (suggested initial value: 1) and `MARKET_RESTOCK_MAX_QUANTITY: int` (suggested initial value: 50) as the tunable restock ceiling used by the restock helper; no other constants changes.
  - src/core/simulation/simulation_engine.gd — wire the new `_tick_market_restock(...)` call into the existing per-tick sequence after `AgentLayer` resolves agent actions, so restocking is applied once per tick after all buy/sell mutations for that tick have completed.
  - src/tests/core/simulation/test_agent_layer.gd — add focused deterministic coverage for: restock increments a depleted commodity quantity by exactly `MARKET_RESTOCK_RATE_PER_TICK`; restock does not increment a commodity that is already at or above `MARKET_RESTOCK_MAX_QUANTITY`; restock applies to both an authored-style dict entry and a procedural-style dict entry in `GameState.locations`; restock does not mutate `buy_price` or `sell_price`.
  - src/tests/core/simulation/test_simulation_tick.gd — add or extend tick-order coverage to confirm that the restock call occurs after agent action resolution within a single tick execution; the test must assert that a depleted market quantity is incremented after one tick fires.
